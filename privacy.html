Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how LCon Games (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the mobile game Worrier Jump (“the App”) on Android. It applies only to this app and not to other apps or websites.

Data controller

The data controller responsible for your information is LCon Games, Lavanterstraße 14c, 9907 Tristach, Austria.

Data we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account / identity: Player name or nickname you enter in the app.
  • Account identifiers: Email or other identifiers you provide (e.g. for support or account linking).
  • Gameplay data: Scores, progress, in-game preferences, and character choices.
  • Analytics: How you use the app, session length, and feature usage (via Firebase) to improve the app.
  • Support: Messages or feedback you send us when you contact support.

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary for the app to function technically and, where applicable, for invoicing or legal requirements.

How we collect data

We collect data:

  • Directly from you: When you enter your player name, email, or other information in the app or when you contact support.
  • Automatically: When you use the app (e.g. gameplay events, usage data).
  • Through third-party services: Google Play (for distribution and related services) and Firebase (for analytics and technical functionality).

Why we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the game (e.g. save progress, show leaderboards).
  • Improve the app (e.g. fix bugs, analyze usage via Firebase).
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and security.

Who we share data with

We may share data with:

  • Google: For distribution via Google Play and related services (e.g. Play Games), and for analytics and technical functionality via Firebase.
  • Legal and authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertising partners.

Data retention

We retain logs and analytics data for 30 days. Account and gameplay data are kept until you delete your account or request deletion. After that, we delete or anonymize data where possible, except where we must keep it for legal obligations.

Your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Object or restrict: Object to certain processing or request that we restrict how we use your data.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority in your country.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section.

Children’s privacy

The App may be used by children of all ages. We take children’s privacy seriously and collect only the lowest possible amount of data necessary for technical functionality and, where required, for invoicing or legal compliance. We do not use your data for personalized advertising. Parents or guardians can contact us at any time to review or request deletion of their child’s data. See Data we collect and Your rights.

International transfers

Your data is processed within the European Union. We do not transfer your personal data to countries outside the EU/EEA.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data (e.g. secure connections, access controls). No system is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new version in the app or on the store listing and on this page. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
